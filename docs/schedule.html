FAQ

Where should I park?
Drive all the way up the steep gravel drive. There will be someone to help you find a spot.
What should I wear?
Comfortable but presentable attire is best — be prepared for a gravel road and an outdoor bonfire. For attire, think family cookout or casual outdoor event. Nice jeans or shorts (if it's hot), and comfortable shoes/sneakers are all okay with us!
What is the food situation?
There will be a Dos Hermanos food truck serving tacos, burritos, and more. Also, plenty of snacks and desserts.
Are there non-alcoholic and alcoholic beverages available?
Yes. For alcoholic beverages, we will be serving beer and wine.
Is there a place to stay nearby?
Yes. Please see the location page on this website for nearby hotels and lodging you can book. There are many options but all will require a drive to and from the venue.
